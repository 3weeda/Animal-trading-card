KOALA

The reason the koala is called a koala bear is because the koala looks like a teddy bear.

  • Scientific name: Phasclarctos Cinereus
  • Average length: 30 inches
  • Average lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: East coast of Australia

Koalas sleep for up to 19 hours. They live and sleep in the eucalyptus trees. They hop from tree to tree and climbs the trees to get leaves to eat. The koala will eat 2.5 pounds of food a day. It uses its claws to get the branches and get the leaves.
